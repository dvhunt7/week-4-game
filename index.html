Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, it will add a specific number to your total.
You win once your total score matches the number you were randomly given at the start.
You lose if you go over the total.
Each time the game starts, it will change the value of each crystal.
